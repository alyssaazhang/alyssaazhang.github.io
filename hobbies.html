athletics

Sports have always been a huge part of who I am, and they've helped me approach all aspects of my life with perseverance and a stronger mentality. Throughout high school as the swim team coach and volleyball team captain, I learned how to lead with a focus on team goals, but also realized how to appreciate each individual's talents for stronger relationships and results.
Swimming has also taught me how to perform under pressure and to never stop working despite mediocre results. I started competitive swimming when I was 6, and although I had to quit at age 11, I went to public lane swims and have since trained alone. The lack of coaching was a significant challenge, but through my consistent dedication, I broke several records at Ontario's highest level of high school swimming and eventually earned a spot on Waterloo's varsity team!
